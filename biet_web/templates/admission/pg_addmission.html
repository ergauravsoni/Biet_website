Eligibility for Admission to M.Tech Courses (2019-2020):

Admission is open to all candidates who have passed B.E./B.Tech examinations (in relevant field) of VTU or any other University recognised by this University or any other examinations recognised by this University as equivalent with not less than 50% (45% in the case of candidates belonging to SC/ST ) of the marks in the aggregate in the examinations of the semester considered for the declaration of class.


The selection is based on the performance in the qualifying examination, written test and personal interview. Preference will be given to candidates qualified in GATE.

Eligibility for Admission to M.C.A Course: (2019-2020):

Passed BCA/Bachelor Degree in Computer Science Engineering or equivalent Degree.

OR

Passed B.Sc./B.Com./B.A. with Mathematics at 10+2 level or at Graduation Level (with additional bridge Courses as per the norms of the concerned University).
Obtained at least 50% marks (45% marks in case of candidates belonging to reserved category) in the qualifying Examination.


Note: PGCET/K MAT/ score is required for Management quota admission also.
