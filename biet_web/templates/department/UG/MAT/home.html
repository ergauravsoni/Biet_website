Department of Mathematics started in the year 1979 in order to cater the needs of mathematical applications for the students pertaining to the Engineering skills. Department of Mathematics has been recognized as Research Centre by Kuvempu University on 08-04-2005. Two candidates have been awarded Ph.D in the research Area : Mathematical Modeling under the supervision of Dr. K.S Basavarajappa Prof. and Head. Later the department of mathematics is recognized as Research Centre by Visvesvaraya Technological university “Jnana Sangama”,(VTU) Belgaum on 25-03- 2008 as Research Centre Five candidates are persuing for Ph.D programme under VTU, Belgaum. Sixteen research papers are published in National and international Journals.


Vision, Mission, PEOs and PSOs

Vision

Our vision is to expand our role as a center for quality graduate and Postgraduate education programs and research in spatial information science and engineering and in information systems generally to help fulfill the needs of engineering education.

Mission

The mission of the Mathematics Department is to have a positive and lasting impact on the quality of mathematics education and assist educators of mathematics in meeting the challenges of today’s dynamic educational landscape.It strives to provide excellent teaching, research, and service to the university community and to the world in the application of mathematics to other disciplines.
