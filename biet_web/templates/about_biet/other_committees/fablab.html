Responsibilities of the this cell:

  • Students/Faculty are to be encouraged to participate in various Innovation related initiatives and competitions organized by MHRD and other organizations.
  • Arrange in house innovation competitions for students.
  • Meet/Interact renowned Business Leaders and top-notch academicians.
  • Create opportunity to nurture and prototype new ideas.
  • Mentoring by industry Professionals.
  • Experiment with new technologies.
  • Visit companies and places and see new culture of Entrepreneurship.

Activities to be conducted in this cell:

  • At least one lecture program/workshop per semester.
  • At least one meeting per semester.
