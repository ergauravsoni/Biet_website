The Hostel was started in 2008 to accommodate BIET girl students, who come from different parts of the world. The hostel now has one block with 4 Floors, accommodating about 300 boarders. The rooms are well furnished and the surroundings of hostel is very green and pleasant, which encourages the students to study. The hostel is having two dining halls to cater to the needs of the boarders for their varied taste. Hostel is known for its discipline and well managed by dedicated faculty & staff members. The hostel is spacious with a built up area of 5029 Sq. Mts. with 84 Rooms accommodating 300 students.


The Chairman of “AMRUTHA”, Dr.S.S.D.J Ladies Hostel is Dr Prabha Mallikarjun, Member ,BEA,Dvg. The warden of the hostel is Dr S M Chandushaker. The hostel is well managed by a team of experienced office staff.


North Indian & south Indian dishes are prepared in the hostel mess. The hostel mess is managed by the hostel administration itself. Ragging is totally prohibited in the hostel. As an entertainment and refreshment hostel has indoor shuttle court, and an indoor sports hall which is used for table tennis, chess, carom board and other indoor games. Common TV hall is facilitated to watch some important program in the limited hours.


Stationery shop facility is provided in the hostel premises. A walking track is also laid all around the hostel. All the national festivals are celebrated in the hostel. The hostel is surrounded by a landscaped garden.


The boarders actively participate in the functions such as freshers’day, Ganesh Chaturthi, Holi, Diwali and fare well function which are held in the hostel every year.
